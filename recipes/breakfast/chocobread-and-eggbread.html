Chocobread and Eggbread

Ingredients:

  • Sliced Bread, 2un
  • Egg, 2un
  • Chocolate bars(your taste), 1un

Steps:

  1. Cut the center of the bread with a cup. Do not rip or throw away the edges, that's part of your Eggbread;
  2. Put a square of chocolate between the couple bread slices;
  3. Fry them, both sides in the frying pan;
  4. Put the edges of the sliced bread in the frying pan, fry both sides;
  5. Put a bit of butter in the bread hole. After that, put an egg inside the holes, and fry them together like that
  6. Get a drink of your choice, and enjoy!
