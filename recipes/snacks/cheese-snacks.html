Cheese Snacks

Ingredients:

  • Bit of Mozzarela Cheese
  • 2 spoons of Maize Starch
  • 1 egg

Steps:

  1. Grate the cheese;
  2. Mix the cheese with the egg and the maize starch;
  3. Really, mix them very well, knead that thing;
  4. Split them in little balls;
  5. Fry the balls in cooking oil or put them in an air fryer, and you're done! Enjoy ;)
