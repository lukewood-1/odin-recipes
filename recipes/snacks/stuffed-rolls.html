Stuffed Rolls

Ingredients:

  • Water, 250ml
  • Milk, 250ml
  • Bit of butter
  • Bit of oil
  • Spices
  • Wheat flour, 300g
  • Anything you wanna stuff the rolls with (processed meat, chesse and ham, sausages, chocolate, idk)
  • Breadcrumbs

Steps:

  1. Mix the water, the milk, the butter, the oil and the spices together;
  2. Then mix the wheat flour with the liquid;
  3. With the flour still hot, split it in pieces with a knife and squish them;
  4. Put the filling of your choice on top of the flour, and close the flour around the filling;
  5. Put two eggs on a bit of milk and mix them well on a container;
  6. Wet the rolls on the mixture, and then roll them on the breadcrumbs;
  7. Fry the rolls on hot cooking oil and you're done! Enjoy!
